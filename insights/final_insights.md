# Final Insights

## Catalog Growth

Netflix experienced significant catalog growth between 2016 and 2020, with a major increase in content additions during this period.

---

## Movies vs TV Shows

Movies represented the majority of the catalog additions throughout the dataset.

TV Shows also showed strong growth, especially after 2017.

---

## International Expansion

International Movies and International TV Shows were among the most frequent categories in the dataset, indicating Netflix's strong global expansion strategy.

The United States appeared as the main production country, while multiple international collaborations became increasingly common.

---

## Content Categories

Drama and International Movies were the most frequent categories for Movies.

For TV Shows, International TV Shows and TV Dramas dominated the catalog.

---

## Dataset Limitations

The country column contains collaborative productions stored in a single field, which required additional treatment using SPLIT and UNNEST.

The listed_in column also stores multiple categories in the same field, making category separation necessary for deeper analysis.
